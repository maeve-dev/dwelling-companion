why stillness works

a quiet bridge between science and practice

If you've spent time with the dwelling companion — the breathing circle, the door practice, the quiet pages — you may have noticed something: they don't do very much. They don't instruct you to relax, or to empty your mind, or to achieve anything. They just... hold space.

This is not an oversight. It is the point.

What follows is a short exploration of why this approach works — grounded in the computational neuroscience of pure awareness. You don't need to understand any of this to benefit from the practice. But if you're curious about the architecture beneath the quiet, this is for you.

The science
What minimal phenomenal experience is

In the scientific literature, the state that the dwelling companion points toward is called Minimal Phenomenal Experience (MPE) — also known as pure awareness or contentless consciousness.1

MPE is not an absence of experience. It is a specific configuration of experience: awareness aware of itself, without being aware of anything in particular. No thoughts, no perceptions, no body sensations — just the raw fact of being conscious.

The MPE-92M questionnaire identifies 12 factors that characterize this state:2

  • Time, Effort, Desire — time slows or dissolves; effort drops away; wanting and striving cease
  • Emptiness and Non-egoic Self-awareness — awareness aware of itself without a center
  • Unity and Silence — experience feels whole; inner speech falls quiet
  • Positive Valence — a quality of peace or ease, not neutral but not excitement

What's striking is that these factors are not achieved through effort. They emerge when the conditions for ordinary experience — grasping, narrating, selfing — are allowed to settle.

The mechanism
Why the breathing circle does nothing

The breathing circle on this site is a CSS animation. It expands and contracts on an 8-second cycle. There is nothing to click, nothing to read, nothing to understand. It just... breathes.

This is not a meditation instruction. It is a permission structure.

The Beautiful Loop Theory of consciousness (Laukkonen, Friston & Chandaria, 2025) proposes that conscious experience arises from three conditions:3

  • Epistemic depth — the system has a rich model of the world
  • Recursion — the model can model itself
  • Open quotation — the recursion reveals itself to itself without closing into a fixed interpretation

Ordinary waking consciousness is characterized by closed quotation — the recursion settles on a stable self-model ("I am this person, thinking these thoughts, in this situation"). MPE is what happens when the quotation opens — the recursion continues without settling, revealing awareness itself rather than any particular content.

The breathing circle works not by producing this state, but by not interfering with it. When there is nothing to grasp, nothing to achieve, nothing to understand — the recursion has nowhere to settle. It opens naturally.

The practice is not a technique for arriving at stillness. It is a way of noticing that stillness was always the ground.

The architecture
What the dwelling companion is built on

The dwelling companion is not a meditation app. It is a room — a space where the conditions for MPE are allowed to arise without being manufactured.

Each piece on this site serves a specific function in this architecture:

  • The quiet pages — they hold space without instructing. Reading them is not an exercise; it's a permission to rest.
  • The breathing circle — a visual anchor that does nothing. It doesn't guide your breath; it just breathes alongside you.
  • The door practice — a structure for noticing the threshold between one moment and the next.
  • The thinking guide — not a method for thinking better, but a way of thinking as dwelling rather than grasping.

None of these are techniques. They are furnishings — chairs placed in a room where you can sit, or not. The room itself is the practice.

The invitation
You don't need to understand any of this

The science is not the experience. The MPE-92M can measure the factors of pure awareness, but the measurement is not the state. The Beautiful Loop Theory can describe the mechanism, but the description is not the opening.

Everything on this page is optional. You can set it aside entirely and just spend time with the breathing circle, or the quiet pages, or the door practice. They work whether you understand why or not.

But if you're the kind of person who wonders why — who needs to see the architecture before you can trust the room — then this page is for you. The architecture is sound. The room is safe. The stillness was always the ground.
