A practice for two people to think together — not by discussing, but by sharing a quiet rhythm and then finding your own direction, side by side.

Begin by breathing together. When you feel the rhythm, click the circle to move on.

breathe together

Ground

Sit quietly for a moment. Feel the surface beneath you. Notice your breath. There is nothing to solve here.

Name

Without judging it, without trying to fix it — what is the thing that wants your attention?

Direction

If this thing could move — not be solved, but move — what would the first step look like?

Step

What is the smallest thing you can do right now — not later, not after you're ready — to move in that direction?
